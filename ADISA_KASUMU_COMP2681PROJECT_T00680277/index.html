The organization is dedicated to providing aid to poor Muslims around the world. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to live a healthy, happy life, and we are committed to making that happen.

Through our various programs and initiatives, we work to provide food, shelter, education, and other essential services to those in need. We are grateful for the support of our donors and volunteers, who make it all possible.

Our Mission

my mission Our mission is to empower poor Muslims worldwide by providing access to essential resources and services that enhance their quality of life and enable them to achieve their full potential. Through our programs and partnerships, we strive alleviate poverty, promote eduton and healthcare, and support the cultural and spiritual values of Islam. We believe that every individual has the right to dignity and respect, and we are committed to building a more just and compassionate global community

Services

    Providing emergency relief and aid in times of crisis or natural disasters, such as food, shelter, medical care, and financial assistance.

    Supporting education and training programs that enable poor Muslims to acquire skills and knowledge that can improve their employment prospects and income.

Get Involved

Learn more about how you can support our cause and make a difference in the lives of poor Muslims.

  • Donating money or other resources, such as food, clothing, or medical supplies, to support the organization's programs and initiatives.
  • Volunteering time and skills to help with the organization's activities, such as organizing events, providing administrative support, or participating in relief efforts.

    • Ongoing Projects

      Partnering with local schools and universities to offer scholarships and financial assistance to poor Muslim students who have the potential to succeed but lack the resources to continue their education.

      Organizing vocational training programs that teach valuable skills and trades, such as carpentry, tailoring, and computer programming, to help young people find employment and improve their income.

      Providing mentoring and support services to help students overcome challenges and obstacles, such as language barriers, cultural differences, and lack of access to resources.

      Working with employers and local businesses to create job opportunities and internships that enable young people to gain practical experience and build their resumes.

      Conducting research and analysis to better understand the needs and challenges facing poor Muslim communities, and to develop more effective and sustainable solutions.
